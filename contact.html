Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our process and services.

How long does a typical website project take?

Most projects take 4-8 weeks depending on complexity. Simple websites can be completed in 2-4 weeks, while complex e-commerce or web applications may take 8-12 weeks.

What's included in your website packages?

All projects include custom design, development, mobile optimization, basic SEO, content management system, and 30 days of post-launch support.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

Yes! We offer monthly maintenance packages including updates, backups, security monitoring, and content changes to keep your site running smoothly.

Can you help with content and copywriting?

Absolutely. We work with experienced copywriters who specialize in conversion-focused content that aligns with your brand voice and business goals.

What if I need changes after the site launches?

We provide 30 days of complimentary post-launch support. After that, we offer flexible maintenance packages or hourly rates for ongoing updates and improvements.

Do you work with clients outside your local area?

Yes, we work with clients worldwide. Most of our communication happens via video calls, email, and project management tools, making location a non-issue.
