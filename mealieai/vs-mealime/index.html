Frequently Asked Questions

What's the main difference between Mealie AI and Mealime?

Mealie AI focuses on AI-powered recipe suggestions and flexible meal planning, while Mealime emphasizes quick, healthy recipes with a focus on dietary restrictions. Mealie AI offers more customization and an AI assistant, while Mealime provides pre-made meal plans.

Is Mealie AI more expensive than Mealime?

Both apps offer free tiers and premium subscriptions. Mealime Pro costs around $5.99/month, while Mealie AI Premium is $6.99/month but includes AI features that Mealime doesn't offer.

Which app has better recipe import?

Mealie AI can import recipes from any website and uses AI to structure them automatically. Mealime has a more limited recipe library and doesn't support importing from external sites.

Can I use my own recipes in both apps?

Mealie AI allows unlimited custom recipes and imports from any URL. Mealime is more focused on their curated recipe library, with limited support for personal recipes.
