Mediterranean Chickpea & Tuna Salad Pitas

A no-cook, protein-packed dinner with chickpeas, tuna, crunchy veggies, and lemony dressing. Stuff into pitas or serve over greens for meal prep.

üìù Ingredients

  • 2 (5 oz) cans tuna in water, drained
  • 1 (15 oz) can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/3 cup red onion, finely diced
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 4 whole-wheat pitas

Instructions

1

In a large bowl, lightly mash half the chickpeas with a fork.

2

Add tuna, remaining chickpeas, cucumber, tomatoes, onion, and parsley.

3

Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, oregano, salt, and pepper; toss with the salad.

4

Stuff into pitas or serve as bowls.

Recipe Notes & Tips

  • Add chopped olives or crumbled feta if your budget allows.
  • For meal prep, keep pita separate to prevent sogginess.
