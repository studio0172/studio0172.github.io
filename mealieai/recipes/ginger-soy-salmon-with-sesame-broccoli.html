Ginger-Soy Salmon with Sesame Broccoli

A quick Asian-inspired dinner with savory-sweet glazed salmon and crisp-tender broccoli. Everything cooks fast, making it ideal for weeknights.

  • 1 1/2 lb salmon fillet, cut into 4 portions
  • 2 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey or brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 2 tsp grated fresh ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 4 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 tbsp neutral oil (canola or avocado)
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 2 cups cooked rice, for serving (optional)
  • Sliced scallions, for serving (optional)

4
