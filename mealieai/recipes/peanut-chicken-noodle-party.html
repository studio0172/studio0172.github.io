üìù Ingredients

Main
  • 250g pack rice noodles
  • 2 ready-cooked chicken breasts, skinned and shredded
Veggies
  • 1 red pepper, deseeded and sliced
  • 2 handfuls beansprouts
Sauces & Toppings
  • 3 tbsp sweet chilli sauce
  • 4 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 4 spring onions, sliced
  • 50g peanuts, roughly chopped
  • 20g fresh coriander, roughly chopped

Instructions

1

Cook noodles & heat chicken

Cook the rice noodles according to the pack instructions. Meanwhile, put the shredded chicken and sliced red pepper in a microwave-proof dish and cover. Microwave on High for 2 minutes, until piping hot.

2

Combine & serve

Drain the rice noodles and tip into a large bowl. Stir through the beansprouts. Whisk the sweet chilli sauce and soy sauce together, then stir through the noodles along with the hot chicken, pepper, and spring onions. Scatter over the chopped peanuts and fresh coriander to serve. Dig in!

  • Veggie version: Swap chicken for extra-firm tofu or edamame for a vegetarian twist.
  • Meal prep friendly: This keeps well in the fridge for 2-3 days ‚Äî just add a splash of soy sauce when reheating.
