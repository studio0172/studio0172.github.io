Lemon Herb Tuna Pasta Salad

üìù Ingredients

  • 12 oz short pasta (rotini or shells)
  • 2 (5 oz) cans tuna in water, drained
  • 1 cup frozen peas (thawed)
  • 1 cup diced cucumber
  • 1 cup halved cherry tomatoes (or diced tomato)
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion (optional)
  • 1/3 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp dried dill or Italian seasoning
  • 3/4 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Instructions

1

Cook pasta in salted water until just tender. Drain and rinse briefly under cool water to stop cooking.

2

In a large bowl, whisk mayonnaise, olive oil, lemon juice, seasoning, salt, and pepper.

3

Add pasta, tuna, peas, cucumber, tomatoes, and onion (if using). Toss until evenly coated.

4

Taste and adjust salt/lemon. Refrigerate at least 20 minutes before eating for best flavor.
