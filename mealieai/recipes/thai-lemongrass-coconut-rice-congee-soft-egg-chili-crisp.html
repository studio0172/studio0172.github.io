Thai Lemongrass Coconut Rice Congee with Soft Egg and Chili Crisp

Silky rice congee scented with lemongrass and ginger, finished with coconut milk for richness. Topped with a soft egg, herbs, and a punchy chili crisp for a brunch bowl that feels special.

Recipe Notes & Tips
