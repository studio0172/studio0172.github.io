Mediterranean Quinoa Bowl

A vibrant, protein-packed bowl featuring fluffy quinoa, crisp vegetables, creamy feta, and a tangy lemon tahini dressing. Perfect for meal prep!

  • 3 tbsp tahini
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

Instructions

1

Cook the quinoa

Combine rinsed quinoa and vegetable broth in a medium saucepan. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes. Remove from heat and let sit covered for 5 minutes, then fluff with a fork.

2

Prepare the vegetables

While the quinoa cooks, dice the cucumber, halve the cherry tomatoes, thinly slice the red onion, and halve the olives. Chop the fresh parsley.

3

Make the lemon tahini dressing

In a small bowl, whisk together tahini, lemon juice, olive oil, minced garlic, and 2 tablespoons of water until smooth and creamy. Add more water if needed to reach desired consistency. Season with salt and pepper.

4

Assemble the bowls

Divide the quinoa between two bowls. Arrange cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, and olives on top. Sprinkle with crumbled feta cheese.

5

Finish and serve

Drizzle generously with lemon tahini dressing and garnish with fresh parsley. Serve immediately or store components separately for meal prep.

Recipe Notes & Tips

  • Meal prep tip: Store quinoa and dressing separately. Keeps in fridge for up to 5 days.
  • Add protein: Top with grilled chicken, chickpeas, or a soft-boiled egg for extra protein.
  • Make it vegan: Simply skip the feta or use a plant-based alternative.
  • Grain swap: Works great with farro, bulgur, or brown rice too.
