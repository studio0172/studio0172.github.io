Honey Garlic Salmon

üìù Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (6 oz each)
  • 4 tbsp honey
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce (or coconut aminos)
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • ¬Ω tsp red pepper flakes
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sesame seeds for garnish
  • Green onions, sliced

Instructions

1

Prep the salmon

Pat salmon fillets dry with paper towels. Season both sides generously with salt and pepper. Let sit at room temperature while you prepare the glaze.

2

Make the honey garlic glaze

In a small bowl, whisk together honey, soy sauce, minced garlic, sesame oil, lemon juice, and red pepper flakes. Set aside.

3

Sear the salmon

Heat olive oil and butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once hot, add salmon fillets skin-side up. Sear for 3-4 minutes until golden brown on the bottom.

4

Add the glaze

Flip the salmon and immediately pour the honey garlic glaze around the fillets (not directly on top). Cook for 4-5 minutes, spooning the glaze over the salmon as it thickens.

5

Finish and serve

The salmon is done when it flakes easily with a fork. Transfer to plates, spoon the remaining glaze over top. Garnish with sesame seeds and sliced green onions. Serve immediately.

Recipe Notes & Tips

  • Salmon selection: Look for wild-caught salmon with bright color and firm flesh. Skin-on fillets work best.
  • Don't overcook: Salmon continues cooking after removed from heat. Pull at 125¬∞F for medium.
  • Serving suggestions: Pairs beautifully with steamed rice, roasted broccoli, or bok choy.
  • Make it low-carb: Swap honey for sugar-free maple syrup for a keto-friendly version.
