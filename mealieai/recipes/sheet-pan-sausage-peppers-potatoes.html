Sheet-Pan Sausage, Peppers & Potatoes

Classic Italian-American flavors with minimal cleanup: smoky sausage, sweet peppers, and crisp potatoes. Perfect for batch cooking and using up leftover veggies.

üìù Ingredients

  • 1 lb Italian sausage links (pork or chicken), sliced into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 1/2 lb baby potatoes, halved
  • 2 bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 large red onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley (optional)
  • 2 tbsp grated Parmesan (optional)

Instructions

1

Heat oven to 425¬∞F. Line a sheet pan with foil or parchment.

2

Toss potatoes with half the olive oil, salt, pepper, oregano, and garlic powder. Spread on the sheet pan and roast 15 minutes.

3

Toss sausage, peppers, and onion with remaining olive oil. Add to the pan, stir, and roast 15 minutes more until sausage is cooked through and veggies are browned.

4

Finish with parsley and Parmesan if using.

Recipe Notes & Tips

  • Cut potatoes small and uniform for even cooking.
  • Use pre-sliced frozen peppers/onions to save time and money.
