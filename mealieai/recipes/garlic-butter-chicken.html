Instructions

1

Pat chicken breasts dry with paper towels ‚Äî this is key for a good sear. Mix salt, pepper, paprika, and garlic powder in a small bowl. Season both sides of each chicken breast evenly.

Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once the oil is shimmering and almost smoking, add chicken breasts. Cook undisturbed for 6-7 minutes until golden brown on the bottom. Don't move them ‚Äî let the crust form!

3

Reduce heat to medium. Add butter to the same skillet. Once melted and foaming, add minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant ‚Äî don't let it brown! Pour in chicken broth and lemon juice, scraping up all those delicious browned bits from the bottom.

5

Finish and serve

Let sauce simmer for 2 minutes until slightly reduced and glossy. Return chicken to the skillet, spooning that gorgeous sauce over the top. Sprinkle generously with fresh parsley and thyme. Serve immediately with your favorite sides.

Serving suggestion: Pairs perfectly with mashed potatoes, rice, or crusty bread to soak up the sauce!
