Indian Spiced Chickpea ‚ÄúChaat‚Äù Jar Salad with Tamarind Date Dressing

üìù Ingredients

  • 2 (15 oz) cans chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 apple, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely diced
  • 1/2 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1/3 cup roasted peanuts, chopped
  • 1/4 cup pomegranate arils (optional)
  • 1 tsp chaat masala
  • 1/2 tsp roasted cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp Kashmiri chili powder (or paprika)
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 3 tbsp tamarind concentrate
  • 2 tbsp date syrup (or 2 tbsp blended dates)
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 1 tbsp neutral oil

Instructions

1

Whisk tamarind, date syrup, lime juice, water, and oil to make the dressing.

2

In a large bowl, combine chickpeas, cucumber, tomatoes, apple, red onion, and cilantro.

3

Add chaat masala, cumin, chili powder, and salt; toss.

4

Portion into jars or containers. Top each with peanuts (and pomegranate if using) and keep dressing separate if you want maximum crunch.

5

Dress and toss right before eating.
