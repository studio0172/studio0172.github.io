Frequently Asked Questions

What's the main difference between Mealie AI and Paprika?

Mealie AI focuses on AI-powered recipe suggestions and smart meal planning, while Paprika is a traditional recipe manager focused on organization and clipping. Mealie AI has an AI assistant that can answer cooking questions and suggest recipes based on ingredients, while Paprika excels at recipe storage and cross-platform sync.

Is Mealie AI more expensive than Paprika?

Paprika uses a one-time purchase model ($4.99 per platform), while Mealie AI uses a subscription model ($6.99/month for Premium). If you use multiple platforms for years, Paprika's one-time cost is cheaper long-term. However, Mealie AI includes AI features that Paprika doesn't offer.

Which app has better recipe import?

Both apps can import recipes from websites. Paprika has been doing this for years with a reliable browser extension. Mealie AI uses AI to structure imported recipes automatically and can handle more complex recipe formats. Both are excellent at importing.

Does Paprika have AI features?

No, Paprika is a traditional recipe manager without AI capabilities. If you want AI-powered suggestions, cooking assistance, or smart recommendations based on your preferences, you'll need Mealie AI or a similar AI-powered app.
