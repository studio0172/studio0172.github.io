Frequently Asked Questions

What's the main difference between Resonant and I Am?

Resonant focuses on personalization‚Äîlearning which affirmations resonate with you over time. I Am emphasizes creating your own custom affirmations with audio recording. Resonant curates content for you; I Am lets you build your own library.

Is Resonant more expensive than I Am?

Both apps offer free versions and premium subscriptions. I Am Premium costs around $9.99/month, while Resonant Premium is typically $4.99/month. Resonant is generally more affordable for the premium tier.

Can I record my own affirmations?

I Am is built around recording your own voice saying affirmations‚Äîthat's its core feature. Resonant focuses on curated, written affirmations that adapt to your preferences but doesn't include voice recording.

Which app is better for beginners?

Resonant is easier for beginners since it provides curated affirmations automatically. I Am requires more effort upfront to record your own content. If you're new to affirmations, Resonant's guided approach may be more accessible.
