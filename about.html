About Studio 0172

An Amazon Freight Partner built on safe miles, dependable schedules, and drivers who take pride in the work.

Who we are

Studio 0172 LLC is an Amazon Freight Partner based in Fremont, California. We move middle-mile freight between Amazon facilities across the Bay Area and the surrounding region — hauling loads with precision, reliability, and genuine care for the schedule we've committed to.

As an Amazon Freight Partner, we're an independently owned and operated trucking company running Class A tractor-trailers in the Amazon Freight Partner program. Every load we take on is a commitment we intend to keep.

Safety is at the core of everything we do. We pair a safety-first mindset with modern equipment and supportive dispatch to make sure our drivers get home safe and every load arrives when it should.

Our people

Our success starts with our drivers. We build a team-oriented culture and look for individuals who strive for excellence in everything they do. From competitive pay and health benefits to incentive bonuses and a fantastic team environment, we invest in our drivers so they can do their best work every single day.

If that sounds like the kind of company you want to drive for, we'd like to hear from you.
