Two Different Scales

üéØ Anxiety Loop: Focused Simplicity

Anxiety Loop does one thing well: help you understand your anxiety patterns. Open the app, tap how you're feeling, see your trends over time. When anxiety spikes, use the built-in breathing exercises to calm down.

That's it. No lessons, no community, no therapy integration. Just a clean, private tool for tracking and awareness. Everything stays on your device‚Äîno account, no cloud, no data leaving your phone.

The philosophy: awareness leads to change. When you see patterns, you start understanding what triggers you and what helps.

üè• Sanvello: Comprehensive Mental Health

Sanvello (formerly Pacifica) is a full mental health platform. It includes mood tracking, but that's just the beginning. The app offers CBT-based thought exercises, guided audio journeys, meditation, a peer community, journaling, goal-setting, and even access to licensed therapists and coaches.

It's designed to be a one-stop mental health solution‚Äîlike having a therapist, meditation app, journal, and community all in one place. For some people, this integration is exactly what they need.

Our Honest Take

Sanvello and Anxiety Loop aren't really competitors‚Äîthey're different categories of app.

Sanvello is trying to be your complete mental health solution. It's ambitious and feature-rich. For people who want guided content, CBT exercises, community support, and even professional therapy access in one place, Sanvello delivers. The tradeoff is complexity, subscription costs, and data privacy concerns.

Anxiety Loop is intentionally minimal. It does one thing‚Äîanxiety tracking‚Äîand does it well. The simplicity is the feature. For people who already have a therapist, or who just want to understand their patterns without committing to another comprehensive platform, Anxiety Loop is refreshingly focused.

If you're struggling and need structured support, Sanvello (or a real therapist) is probably more appropriate. If you want a simple awareness tool to complement other resources, Anxiety Loop fits perfectly.

Privacy-conscious users should note: this isn't close. Anxiety Loop's on-device architecture means your anxiety data stays yours. Sanvello requires cloud storage by design.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the main difference between Anxiety Loop and Sanvello?

Anxiety Loop is a focused anxiety tracker with one-tap check-ins and breathing exercises‚Äîall data stays on your device. Sanvello is a comprehensive mental health platform with CBT tools, therapy options, community features, and guided content‚Äîrequiring an account and cloud storage.

Which app is more private?

Does Sanvello offer therapy?

Yes, Sanvello offers coaching and therapy options as part of their premium subscription. This includes access to licensed therapists through the app. Anxiety Loop is not a therapy platform and focuses solely on tracking and awareness.

Sanvello offers a free tier with limited features. Full access to all content, therapy, and coaching requires a premium subscription at $8.99/month or $53.99/year. Anxiety Loop is completely free with all features included.

Can I use both apps together?

Yes! Many people use Anxiety Loop for simple, private pattern tracking while using Sanvello for CBT exercises or community support. The apps serve different purposes and complement each other.
