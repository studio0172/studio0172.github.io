Who Anxiety Loop Is For

Anxiety Loop is designed for anyone who wants to understand their anxiety better through simple, consistent tracking.

  • People who experience occasional or frequent anxiety and want to notice patterns
  • Anyone curious about how their mood changes day-to-day or week-to-week

  • Anyone looking for a non-clinical way to reflect on their emotional state

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a therapy or medical app?

No. Anxiety Loop is a wellness companion for tracking and reflection—not a replacement for therapy, medical advice, or professional mental health treatment. If you're struggling, please reach out to a mental health professional.

Is my data stored online?

No. All your data stays on your device. We don't have servers that store your check-ins, patterns, or any personal information. Your mental health data belongs to you.

Do I need an account?

No account required. Just download the app and start tracking. No email, no password, no sign-up process.

How long does a check-in take?

About 2 seconds. Tap the mood that matches how you're feeling, and you're done. That's it—no lengthy questionnaires or complicated inputs.
