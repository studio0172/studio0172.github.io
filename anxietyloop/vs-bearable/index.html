Frequently Asked Questions

What's the main difference between Anxiety Loop and Bearable?

Anxiety Loop is focused purely on anxiety tracking with one-tap check-ins. Bearable is a comprehensive symptom and health tracker designed for chronic conditions, tracking mood, symptoms, medications, and potential triggers. Bearable is much more detailed; Anxiety Loop is intentionally simple.

Which app is better for chronic illness?

Bearable is specifically designed for chronic illness tracking with medication logs, symptom tracking, and correlations. Anxiety Loop focuses only on anxiety levels and wouldn't replace a comprehensive health tracker.

Is Anxiety Loop simpler to use?

Yes, significantly. Anxiety Loop is one tap to check in. Bearable offers extensive tracking options which means more data entry. Choose based on whether you need comprehensive tracking or focused simplicity.

Does Bearable have breathing exercises?

No, Bearable focuses on tracking and correlations. Anxiety Loop includes guided breathing exercises for immediate anxiety relief.
