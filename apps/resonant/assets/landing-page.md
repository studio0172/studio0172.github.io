# Resonant

**Daily affirmations that learn what moves you.**

---

## Features

### Personalized Affirmations

Your affirmations adapt to you. Resonant learns from your interactions—what you save, what you return to, what hits home—and surfaces content that genuinely resonates. No algorithms gaming your attention. Just quiet intelligence that gets better over time.

---

### 700+ Handcrafted Affirmations

Every affirmation is thoughtfully written across **8 themes** and **5 tones**:

**Themes**
- Anxiety & Calm
- Self-Trust
- Letting Go
- Uncertainty
- Boundaries
- Patience
- Resilience
- Grounding

**Tones**
- Stoic — calm, restrained, grounded
- Modern — direct, contemporary
- Soft — gentle, nurturing
- Reflective — thoughtful, lyrical
- Practical — steady, action-oriented

---

### Immersive Reading Mode

Long-press any affirmation to enter a distraction-free, full-screen experience. Just you and the words.

---

### Save What Resonates

Tap the heart to save affirmations that speak to you. Mark ones that truly hit home. Your saved collection becomes a personal library of lines worth returning to.

---

### Gentle Reminders

Set multiple daily reminders at times that work for you—morning, midday, evening. Each notification delivers an affirmation selected just for you.

---

### Beautiful Themes

Choose from handcrafted visual themes that shift subtly with the time of day:

- Ocean
- Serenity
- Forest
- Midnight
- Daylight

---

### Home Screen & Lock Screen Widgets

Keep affirmations visible throughout your day. Widgets available in multiple sizes for your home screen and lock screen. Tap to open directly in the app.

---

### Explore by Category

Browse affirmations by theme. Filter by tone. Find exactly what you need, when you need it.

---

### Privacy First

- All data stays on your device by default
- No account required
- No tracking, no ads, no data selling
- Optional iCloud sync—your choice

---

### Gesture-Driven

- Swipe up or left → Next affirmation
- Swipe down → Previous affirmation
- Swipe right → Save
- Long press → Immersive mode

---

### Morning Intention

Start each day by choosing a theme to focus on. A small ritual that sets the tone.

---

### Dark Mode

Full support for light and dark appearances, adapting seamlessly to your system preference.

---

### Accessibility

Built with accessibility in mind—screen reader support, reduce motion options, and adaptive text sizing.

---
