# Resonant - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 21, 2025

Studio0172 ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Resonant mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

Your privacy is important to us. We designed Resonant with privacy in mind, keeping your data on your device and under your control.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following information locally on your device:

- **Preferences:** Your selected theme, notification settings, and app preferences
- **Saved Affirmations:** Affirmations you mark as favorites
- **Interaction Data:** Which affirmations you've viewed, marked as "Hits Home," or interacted with
- **Onboarding Responses:** Your selections during the initial setup process

This data is stored solely on your device and is used to personalize your experience within the App.

### 1.2 iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync in the App settings:

- Your saved affirmations and preferences may be stored in your personal iCloud account
- This data syncs across your Apple devices signed into the same iCloud account
- We do not have access to your iCloud data; it is managed entirely by Apple

### 1.3 Subscription Information

If you purchase a Premium subscription:

- Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store
- We receive confirmation of your subscription status from Apple
- We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID

### 1.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Contacts or address book information
- Photos or media
- Health data
- Browsing history
- Device identifiers for advertising purposes
- Any data from other apps on your device

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

The information stored locally on your device is used to:

- **Personalize Content:** Learn your preferences to show more relevant affirmations
- **Save Favorites:** Remember which affirmations you've saved
- **Maintain Preferences:** Keep your theme, notification, and app settings
- **Improve Experience:** Track which categories resonate with you to enhance recommendations

We do not use your information for advertising, marketing, or any purpose other than providing and improving the App experience.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Local Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS data storage mechanisms. This data is protected by your device's security features, including:

- Device passcode/Face ID/Touch ID
- iOS encryption
- App sandboxing

### 3.2 iCloud Storage

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is protected by Apple's iCloud security measures. For more information, see [Apple's iCloud Security Overview](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT202303).

### 3.3 No Server Storage

We do not operate servers that store your personal data. Your information remains on your device (and optionally in your personal iCloud account).

---

## 4. Data Sharing

### 4.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties.

### 4.2 Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

**Apple App Store & StoreKit**
- Purpose: Process subscription purchases
- Data shared: Subscription status only
- Privacy Policy: [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

**Apple iCloud (Optional)**
- Purpose: Sync data across your devices
- Data shared: Your app preferences and saved items (only if you enable sync)
- Privacy Policy: [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

### 4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency). However, since we do not collect personal data on our servers, we have minimal information to disclose.

---

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Access Your Data

All your data is stored on your device. You can view your saved affirmations and preferences directly within the App.

### 5.2 Delete Your Data

You can delete your data at any time by:

- **Within the App:** Go to Settings → Reset to clear all local data
- **Deleting the App:** Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data
- **iCloud Data:** Disable iCloud sync in settings, or delete the App's iCloud data through your device's iCloud settings

### 5.3 Control iCloud Sync

You can enable or disable iCloud sync at any time in the App's settings.

### 5.4 Manage Notifications

You can enable, disable, or customize notification times in the App's settings or through your device's notification settings.

### 5.5 Opt Out

Since we don't collect personal data on external servers, there's no need to opt out of data collection. Simply delete the App to remove all data.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since the App does not collect personal information and stores data only locally, there is minimal risk to children's privacy.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can address your concerns.

## 9. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have certain rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

- **Right to Know:** You can request information about the personal data we collect. As stated above, we do not collect personal data on our servers.
- **Right to Delete:** You can delete your data by resetting the App or uninstalling it.
- **Right to Non-Discrimination:** We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

Since we do not collect, sell, or share personal information as defined by the CCPA, most CCPA provisions do not apply to our data practices.

---

## 10. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

- **Right of Access:** Your data is stored locally and accessible to you at any time.
- **Right to Rectification:** You can modify your preferences within the App.
- **Right to Erasure:** You can delete your data through Settings → Reset or by uninstalling the App.
- **Right to Data Portability:** Your data is stored in standard formats on your device.
- **Right to Object:** Since we don't process your data on external servers, this right is not applicable.

**Legal Basis for Processing:** The data stored locally on your device is processed based on your consent (by using the App) and is necessary to provide the App's functionality.

---

## 11. App Tracking Transparency

Resonant does not track you across other companies' apps or websites. We do not use the Advertising Identifier (IDFA) or any other tracking technologies for advertising purposes.

If prompted by iOS, you can safely deny tracking permission as the App does not utilize this functionality.
