Privacy Policy

Resonant - Daily Affirmations

Last Updated: January 21, 2025

Studio0172 ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Resonant mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

In short: Your data stays on your device. We don't collect it, we don't sell it, and we don't share it.

1. Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

  • Preferences: Your selected theme, notification settings, and app preferences
  • Saved Affirmations: Affirmations you mark as favorites
  • Interaction Data: Which affirmations you've viewed, marked as "Hits Home," or interacted with
  • Onboarding Responses: Your selections during initial setup

This data is stored solely on your device to personalize your experience.

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync:

  • Your saved affirmations and preferences may be stored in your personal iCloud account
  • Data syncs across your Apple devices signed into the same iCloud account
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data—it is managed entirely by Apple

Subscription Information

If you purchase a Premium subscription:

  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store
  • We receive confirmation of your subscription status from Apple
  • We do not collect or have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book information
  • Photos or media
  • Health data
  • Browsing history
  • Device identifiers for advertising

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Personalize Content: Learn your preferences to show more relevant affirmations
  • Save Favorites: Remember which affirmations you've saved
  • Maintain Preferences: Keep your theme, notification, and app settings
  • Improve Experience: Track which categories resonate with you

We do not use your information for advertising or marketing.

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Your data is stored locally using standard iOS data storage mechanisms, protected by:

  • Device passcode/Face ID/Touch ID
  • iOS encryption
  • App sandboxing

No Server Storage

We do not operate servers that store your personal data. Your information remains on your device (and optionally in your personal iCloud account).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

The App uses these third-party services:

  • Apple App Store & StoreKit: Process subscription purchases. Apple Privacy Policy
  • Apple iCloud (Optional): Sync data across your devices if enabled. Apple Privacy Policy

5. Your Rights and Choices

Access Your Data

All your data is stored on your device. You can view your saved affirmations and preferences directly within the App.

Delete Your Data

  • Within the App: Go to Settings → Reset to clear all local data
  • Deleting the App: Uninstalling removes all locally stored data
  • iCloud Data: Disable iCloud sync in settings or delete via your device's iCloud settings

Control iCloud Sync

You can enable or disable iCloud sync at any time in the App's settings.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since the App stores data only locally, there is minimal risk to children's privacy.

7. International Users

The App is available internationally through the Apple App Store. Your data remains on your device regardless of your location. If you use iCloud sync, your data may be transferred to Apple data centers subject to Apple's privacy practices.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you by updating the "Last Updated" date and providing notice within the App for significant changes.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have certain rights under the CCPA. Since we do not collect, sell, or share personal information as defined by the CCPA, most provisions do not apply to our data practices. You can delete your data by resetting the App or uninstalling it.

10. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EEA, you have data protection rights under GDPR:

  • Right of Access: Your data is stored locally and accessible to you at any time
  • Right to Rectification: You can modify your preferences within the App
  • Right to Erasure: Delete your data through Settings → Reset or by uninstalling
  • Right to Data Portability: Your data is stored in standard formats on your device

11. App Tracking Transparency

Resonant does not track you across other apps or websites. We do not use the Advertising Identifier (IDFA) or any tracking technologies for advertising purposes.
