FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do I apply to become a driver?

Head to our Careers page and click "Apply Today" to submit your application. Our team reviews every application and will reach out about next steps.

What are the requirements to drive for Studio 0172?

You'll need a valid Class A commercial driver's license, a good driving record, and the ability to meet DOT requirements for a safety-sensitive driving position. We provide training to set you up for success.

What is an Amazon Freight Partner?

Amazon Freight Partners are independent trucking companies that move freight between Amazon facilities — the middle mile — using Class A tractor-trailers. Studio 0172 LLC is an independently owned and operated company participating in that program. This is freight hauling, not last-mile package delivery.

What benefits do you offer?

We offer highly competitive pay, health benefits, paid time off, incentive bonuses, and a fantastic team environment.

What area does Studio 0172 serve?

We operate out of Fremont, CA and run freight lanes across the Bay Area and surrounding region as an Amazon Freight Partner.

Ready to get on the road?

We're hiring Class A CDL drivers who take pride in doing the job safely and well. We'd like to hear from you.
