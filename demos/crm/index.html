FAQ

Questions? Answers.

Is this really free?
Early access is completely free. We're building in public and want real feedback from real teams. Paid tiers will come later with advanced features—but the core will always be accessible.
Can I import my existing data?
Yes. Export your spreadsheet as CSV and we'll help you import. Most teams are up and running in under an hour.
How is this different from other CRMs?
We built this for teams that need a CRM but don't need Salesforce. No complex setup. No features you'll never use. Just the essentials, done well.
What integrations do you support?
Email (Gmail, Outlook), calendar sync, and Zapier for everything else. We're adding more based on user requests.
